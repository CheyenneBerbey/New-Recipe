Healthy Banana Pancakes

Servings:
4 to 6
Time:
15 minutes

These Pancakes are so healthy and easy to make, your whole family is going to want to know the secret to these mouth watering cakes..

Ingredients

  • 2 Large bananas, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla
  • 1 Whole Egg
  • 1 pinch cinnamon
  • 2 Egg whites

Preparation

  1. Heat the oven top to medium heat.
  2. Mush Peeled bananas in seperate bowl with fork ¼ to ½ add oil.
  3. Mix Eggs in a bowl with mashed bananas, and sprinkle the pinch of cinnamon in as well as the tablespoon of vanilla.
  4. Pour 1 cup of pancake batter in medium sized pan three times.
  5. Let pancakes cook for about 4 minutes, then flip and cook until the other sides edges are cooked enough to filp.Serve hot.
